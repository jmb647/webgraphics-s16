Welcome to Algarve Agency

About Us

Algarve Agency is the leading Portuguese airline, it has been flying since 1945 and has been a member of the Star Alliance since 14 March 2005. Its Lisbon hub is a key European gateway at the crossroads of Africa, and North and South America.With the launch of new routes over recent years, Algarve Agency’s summer 2014 network grew to include 11 new destinations. Currently the Portuguese airline has connections to 81 destinations in 34 countries. It operates an average of around 2500 flights a week and has a fleet of 77 modern aircraft: 61 Airbuses, and another 16 aircraft in the colours of PGA (Portugália Airlines).

Things To Do

Kayaking

We begin our day paddling the Aveiro city channels and then get out trough a water lock and get lost in the multitude of the lagoon. We will feel the lush green valley, see the beautiful houses and Quintas. At the end of the paddling day we will drop you again.. on a train station so you will be back to Oporto at dinner time. 5 hours tour including all the gear, guide and light lunch.

Snorkeling

On the beach we provide snorkel equipment to make your Kayak & Snorkel Trip an even better experience. There is no rush, we normally stay at least half an hour at the beach swimming, snorkeling, relaxing and chatting. he beaches we visit are a bit off the beaten path, so you won’t need to share them with a terribly large number of people. In fact, you’ll practically have them all to yourself.

Scuba Diving

There’s an incredible variety of marine life waiting to be discovered in the waters off the Algarve coast. At Algarve Divexperience we have explored the coastline for many years and are happy to share our expertise, guiding you to the most interesting local dive locations.
